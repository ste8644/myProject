Rules of Ice Hockey

There are many rules in ice hockey, ranging from simple to very complicated. These rules are enforced in all scenarios, and most are at the referee's discretion. If you do not follow these rules given, you could recive the punishment of anything from a 2:00 minute minor penalty to suspension for the remainder of the season. These rules are taken very seriously by leagues, in able to keep the game safe and playable. Below, you can use this link to see a list of all the rules of hockey. List of Hockey Rules

However, there are some ways to "bend" these rules or avoid a penalty.

Rule/Penalty How to avoid
Offsides When you are getting close to being offsides, skate directly parallel to the blue line
Tripping Make sure you keep your feet moving and that the other player is in control of the puck
