Rules of Ice Hockey

There are many rules in ice hockey, ranging from simple to very complicated. These rules are enforced in all scenarios, and most are at the referee's discretion. If you do not follow these rules given, you could recive the punishment of anything from a 2:00 minute minor penalty to suspension for the remainder of the season. These rules are taken very seriously by leagues, in able to keep the game safe and playable.

However, there are ways to "bend" these rules
