Rules of Ice Hockey


There are many rules in ice hockey, ranging from simple to very complicated. These rules are enforced in all scenarios, and most are at the referee's discretion. If you do not follow these rules given, you could recive the punishment of anything from a 2:00 minute minor penalty to suspension for the remainder of the season. These rules are taken very seriously by leagues, in able to keep the game safe and playable. At the bottom of this page, you can see a list of the basic rules and penalties of hockey if you are not already familliar with the game. You can also use this link to learn about the game if you are not familliar with it:Ice Hockey

However, there are some ways to "bend" these rules or avoid a penalty.

Rule/Penalty How to avoid
Offsides When you are getting close to being offsides, skate directly parallel to the blue line.
Tripping Make sure you keep your feet moving and that the other player is in control of the puck.
Cross Check Keep your hands low and don't extend your arms.
10 minute misconduct Whatever the referee says, just say yes sir, don't talk back in a rude way, or do anything to insult him or make him look bad.
Holding Keep your hands low and don't pull him back or do anything too obvious.
